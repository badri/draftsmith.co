The Draftsmith Roadmap*

*Subject to change, detours, and sudden bursts of inspiration.

1

"Just Blog It"

Now

Write beautiful blog posts without monthly fees, in the comfort of your own computer.

2

"Dear Internet, It's Me"

Support for emails, tweets, and LinkedIn posts. Because sometimes you need to overshare in multiple formats.

3

"Talk Pretty to Me"

Support for multiple languages. Draftsmith will finally understand you when you rant in Spanish, whisper in French, or write angry haikus in Japanese.

4

"The Rise of the Machines"

Agents. Your own little writing minions that brainstorm, draft, and maybe plot world domination (but mostly just content).

Remember: This roadmap is more of a "rough sketch on a napkin" than a legally binding contract. I reserve the right to pivot, procrastinate, or get distracted by shiny new features.
